Tomato Sauce

Yummy tomato sauce! Yummy!

  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Sugar
  • Lemon
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Oregano
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  1. Chop up and cook garlic and onion in olive oil.
  2. Add canned tomates, taste with sugar and lemon and oregano.
  3. Let it cook for around 20 - 30 minutes. Taste with salt and pepper.
